**Multimodal AI**

https://lnkd.in/dPpVAStM

Multimodal AI refers to systems that process text, images, and audio at the same time—meaning they can look at a photo, hear a question, and answer with a coherent description. Since 2025, we’ve seen a wave of models that truly blend modalities: GPT-4o (OpenAI) combines voice, vision, and text in real time; Gemini (Google) works across text, images, video, and audio; Claude 3 (Anthropic) handles multimodal input with extremely long context windows; and Grok (xAI) merges live web search with content generation. Video creation is already possible with Sora, while TranslateGemma supports 55 languages and can translate text embedded in images. Multimodality now sits under the hood of assistants (Copilot, Amazon Q), image generators (DALL·E, Midjourney), audio tools (ElevenLabs), and meeting apps (Otter.ai). The market is taking off—analysts expect annual growth north of 30%. Use these tools, but don’t take everything at face value: verify facts and guard your privacy.
